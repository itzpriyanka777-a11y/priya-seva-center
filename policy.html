To ensure smooth operations for our independent contractors, the following policies apply:

Clients are granted up to 3 unpaid absence days per month. Additional leaves will be billed at the standard daily rate to compensate the reserved contractor. If a contractor fails to arrive, mark "Worker Absent" to guarantee you are not charged.
