Legal & Privacy Protocol

Effective Date: May 2026 | Governing Jurisdiction: Kolkata, West Bengal

1. Data Encryption & Privacy

Your security is our primary directive. Priya Seva Center utilizes state-of-the-art decentralized vault storage provided by Supabase. Your authentication credentials, operational timelines, and location data are end-to-end encrypted.

We strictly strictly adhere to data protection regulations. We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information, service history, or medical notes to any third-party marketing entities.

2. The 48-Hour Cancellation Rule

To ensure precision deployment of our field personnel, all service modifications are governed by the 48-Hour Protocol.

Standard Operations: Any edits or cancellations made greater than 48 hours prior to the scheduled service time are processed without penalty via your Secure Dashboard.

Locked Window: Cancellations or modifications requested within 48 hours of the service start time are automatically locked by the system to prevent dispatch routing errors. Such changes require manual administrative authorization and may be subject to a late dispatch fee.

3. Absence Logging & Billing Policy

We understand that life is unpredictable. If you are traveling or unavailable to receive your recurring service, you must log a "Client Absence" via our Resolution Center. Our transparent absence billing structure is as follows:

  • Standard Absences: Clients are permitted up to three (3) free "Client Absent" logs per calendar month without incurring service cycle charges.
  • Emergency Absences: Clients are granted an additional two (2) free emergency absences per month, subject to review.
  • Billing Threshold: Any absences exceeding the combined free allowance will be billed at the standard daily operational rate to compensate our reserved personnel.

If an agent fails to arrive ("Agent Absent"), you must log it immediately. You will never be billed for an unrendered service resulting from an agent no-show.

4. Liability Disclaimer

Priya Seva Center acts as a premium coordination grid connecting clients with verified personnel. While we enforce rigorous background and medical credential checks:

  • Medical Non-Liability: The "Health Visits" and "Certified Nurse" deployments provide clinical support, but Priya Seva Center is not a substitute for an emergency room. In case of acute medical emergencies, please dial emergency services immediately.
  • Contractor Protocol: Personnel dispatched are registered professionals operating under the Priya Seva Center network. Any disputes regarding specific operational execution should be logged immediately via the Grievance Sub-Portal for management intervention.

5. Grievance & Dispute Resolution

We hold our network to the highest standard of excellence. If an incident occurs, clients are required to file a report via the "File a Grievance" modal available in the menu. Our administrative desk aims to investigate and initiate a resolution sequence within 24 hours of submission.
